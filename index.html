Instructions

  1. Hit the start button which starts a 30 seconds countdown.
  2. Objective is to connect starting pipe (orange dot) to ending pipe (red dot).
  3. Move tiles by clicking on tile and empty space
  4. Once timer is up, tiles are randomized and you have to start all over.
  5. Each move is recorded (the lesser moves made to solve, the better).
  6. Levels reduce the time given to solve puzzle, and lesser space to move.
